Music Together has been known for making rich musical experiences accessible to families for years. Music Together Online will offer the same content through slightly different channels. Shorter, more frequent recorded classes allow you to access music when it works best for you. Weekly live classes allow you to interact with your teacher and other families!

Concerned that your child won't sit and watch? They don't need to! We know that children learn from their close care givers and they pick things up from all around them. Your child doesn't need to be watching the screen, they just need to be near you (even if it seems like they aren't paying attention to you or the screen) while you participate in order to benefit from our online classes.
