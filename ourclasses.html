Our 45 minute classes are held weekly and can accommodate up to 12 students and their parent or caretaker. During each class, your trained instructor will lead you and your child through a series of music and movement activities, each designed to inspire the natural development of rhythm, tonal awareness.

Singing, dancing, drumming and humming along is not only allowed - it's part of the program! The more you participate, the more your child will want to join in the fun.

Your enrollment in our 10-week session includes admission to one weekly class for you and your child, a beautifully illustrated songbook, 2 copies of the CD (per family) used in class throughout the semester plus access to the Family Music Zone where you can find fun resources and stream or download music. Families new to Music Together also receive a parent guide DVD. Each semester you will take home a new, exciting collection of songs. Families who attend Fall, Winter, and Spring semesters for three years will collect the complete set of nine Music Together songbooks and recordings.

In every class, you and your child will have the opportunity to play along to music on our age-appropriate, professional-grade instruments that include hand drums, bells, rhythm sticks, shakers and more. This provides a fun and informal exposure to the world of musical instruments.

Interested in coming to check out one of our classes for free? Click the orange button on the left to schedule your attendance to a free demo class.

There is no obligation, simply come and enjoy one of our fun mixed-age classes.

Note - space in demo classes may be limited and, unfortunately, we cannot accept drop-ins. Please schedule in advance.

Each 10-week session has a cost of $190 per student. A one-time fee of $20 is charged for first time families. We offer a reduced cost $140 for each additional sibling who attends the same class.

Note: Summer Sessions may be offered in 4, 5, or 6 week options - see Registration Page for details and pricing.

We encourage all families to attend a FREE Demo Class prior to enrolling. Unfortunately, we cannot offer a refund on tuition.
