Overview

Podcast advertising and marketing is rapidly becoming the best option for many brands who need the ability to quickly reach a targeted audience. Podcast listeners are among the most qualified consumers an advertiser can ask for. Alkendesign Marketing and Media is an independent consultancy that works directly with several of today’s most popular podcasts. We differ greatly from a traditional advertising agency in that our service is designed to help you leverage the entirety of our clients’ outlets, including web banners, social media, email list and more.

Why Podcast Advertising Works

The effectiveness of podcast advertising can be attributed to a wide variety of factors, but the biggest is the connection between the celebrity/podcaster and their audience. Listeners have actively chosen to seek, download, and set aside time to enjoy each broadcast. This creates an intimacy unlike any other medium. For an advertiser, this type of connection in tremendously valuable.

Other factors include:

  • Qualified and eager consumers
  • Unparalleled metrics
  • Focused audience targeting
  • Ad reads remain live indefinitely
