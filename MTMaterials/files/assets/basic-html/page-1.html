Music Together In School :

Curriculum and Support

Through nearly 30 years of ongoing research and development, Music Together has carefully

selected a mix of resources to provide a developmentally appropriate early childhood music

and movement curriculum supporting both music and other key developmental domains.

Parents and educators find the curriculum accessible, regardless of their own musical ability;

and school administrators appreciate the abundant support, which includes new materials

for each term in the rotation of our nine song collections.
